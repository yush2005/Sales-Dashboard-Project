# Sales-Dashboard-Project
Advanced Sales Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI to visualize revenue, profit, customer insights, and business performance.
Project Overview

This project showcases a multi-page interactive Sales Dashboard developed in Power BI. It provides a complete analysis of business performance across revenue, profit, products, channels, and geography.

The dashboard is divided into three main sections:

Executive Overview & Trends
Product & Channel Performance
Geographic & Customer Insights
🚀 Dashboard Features
🔹 1. Executive Overview & Trends
KPI Cards:
Total Revenue: $1.2B
Total Profit: $461.8M
Profit Margin: 37.36%
Total Orders: 64K
Revenue per Order: $19.3K
Monthly revenue and profit trend analysis
Order value distribution (customer spending behavior)
Price vs profit margin analysis
🔹 2. Product & Channel Performance
Top-selling products driving revenue
High-margin products analysis
Revenue vs Profit scatter analysis
Channel contribution (Wholesale, Distributor, Export)
Profit pipeline and efficiency comparison
🔹 3. Geographic & Customer Insights
Top customers by revenue
Top states contributing to sales (e.g., California leading)
Regional revenue and profit margin distribution
Map visualization of profit by location
📈 Key Insights
California contributes ~19.5% of total revenue
West region has the highest profit margin (~37.5%)
Certain products generate high revenue but lower margins (optimization opportunity)
Export channel shows strong profitability contribution
