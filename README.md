# 📊 Advanced Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## 🧾 Overview
This project showcases a multi-page **Sales Analytics Dashboard** built using Power BI. It provides deep insights into revenue, profit, customer behavior, product performance, and geographic trends to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🚀 Dashboard Sections

### 🔹 1. Executive Overview & Trends
- KPI Cards:
  - Total Revenue: $1.2B
  - Total Profit: $461.8M
  - Profit Margin: 37.36%
  - Total Orders: 64K
  - Revenue per Order: $19.3K
- Monthly revenue and profit trend analysis
- Order value distribution (customer spending behavior)
- Unit Price vs Profit Margin analysis

---

### 🔹 2. Product & Channel Performance
- Top-selling products driving revenue
- High-margin products analysis
- Revenue vs Profit scatter analysis
- Channel contribution (Wholesale, Distributor, Export)
- Profit pipeline and channel efficiency comparison

---

### 🔹 3. Geographic & Customer Insights
- Top customers by revenue
- Top states contributing to sales (e.g., California leading)
- Regional revenue distribution
- Profit margin by region
- Map visualization of profit by location

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- California contributes ~19.5% of total revenue
- West region has the highest profit margin (~37.5%)
- High-revenue products do not always yield high profit margins
- Export channel contributes significantly to profitability
